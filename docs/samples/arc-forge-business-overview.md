## Key Differentiators

1. **Business Context Layer**: Unlike Xero's JAX (which only knows your numbers), Pip knows your business plans, KPIs, and strategy. This enables answering questions like "Can I afford to hire?" that require both financial data AND business context.

2. **Platform Agnostic**: Future-proofed to work across Xero, MYOB, QuickBooks - switch accounting software without losing your AI assistant's history.

3. **Open Source**: Built on open source stack, portable, no vendor lock-in.

## Target Market

**Primary Avatar**: Small business owner managing own books
- 0-5 employees

- Using Xero, doing books themselves
