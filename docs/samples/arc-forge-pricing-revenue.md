# Arc Forge Pricing & Revenue Model

## Current Phase: Beta Validation

### Beta Terms
- **Max users**: 25 Xero connections
- **Price**: Free (absorbing token costs)
- **Distribution**: Manual approval only
- **Purpose**: Validate usefulness, collect feedback

### Why Free Beta?
- Need real user feedback before pricing
- Building testimonials and social proof
- Token costs are minimal at 25 users (~$1-2/month)
- Price sensitivity research during demo

## Target Pricing (Post-Validation)

### Consumer Tier
| Tier | Monthly Price | Target Customer |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Starter | $29/month | Solo operator, basic needs |
| Pro | $49/month | Small team, multiple users |

### Why This Price Point?
- Bookkeeper costs $400-800/month - we're 10x cheaper
- "Cheaper than one hour of your accountant" messaging
- Sweet spot identified in avatar research ($20-50/month)
- Room to increase as features expand

## Revenue Sprint Goals

### Immediate Goal: $250/week profit
- Mix of freelance services + Pip subscriptions
- Validates business model sustainability

### Scenario Analysis

**10 paying users at $29/month**:
- Revenue: $290/month ($72.50/week)
- Not enough alone - needs freelance supplement

**25 paying users at $29/month**:
- Revenue: $725/month ($181.25/week)
- Close to target with low support overhead

**25 users at $49/month**:
- Revenue: $1,225/month ($306.25/week)
- Exceeds target - sustainable solo operation

## Cost Structure

### Operating Costs (Monthly)
| Item | Cost | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| VPS hosting | $0 | Shared droplet (already paid) |
| Domain (askpip.au) | ~$3 | Amortized annual |
| Anthropic API | $0.50-2 | Claude Haiku, depends on usage |
| Xero app fee | $0 | Free for app partners |
| **Total** | ~$5/month | |

### Token Cost Analysis
- Claude Haiku: ~$0.0015 per query
- 25 users × 10 queries/day × 30 days = 7,500 queries/month
- Monthly API cost: ~$11.25
- Per-user cost: ~$0.45/month

### Gross Margin Target
- At $29/month: $29 - $0.45 = $28.55 (98% margin)
- At $49/month: $49 - $0.45 = $48.55 (99% margin)
